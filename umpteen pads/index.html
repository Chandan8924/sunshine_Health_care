Sanitary pads are an essential product for women's healthcare during menstruation. They are designed to absorb menstrual blood and prevent leaks, providing comfort and protection. Proper use of sanitary pads can help prevent infection, skin irritation, and other menstrual health issues. It is essential to change pads regularly, usually every 4-6 hours, to maintain good hygiene and prevent unpleasant odors. Using high-quality, comfortable, and properly fitting sanitary pads can help women manage their menstruation with ease and confidence, promoting their overall health and well-being. Women's healthcare refers to the medical and preventive services aimed at addressing women's unique health needs. These needs include reproductive health, maternal health, prenatal care, gynecological care, breast health, and menopause management. It also involves screening and managing gender-specific health issues such as cervical and breast cancer, sexually transmitted infections, and domestic violence.
